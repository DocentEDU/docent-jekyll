Features

Save Time

Create and assign a lesson in minutes. Find your text, add questions, assign. It’s that simple

Engage Students

Add discussions, videos, questions, and interactive content from other sites directly to any webpage

Assess

Assess student learning with different question types, quizzes, and auto-grading.

Class Discussions

Turn text into private whole class discussions that are focused on specific topics.


Google Integration

Google Sign-In

Safe and secure Google log in. Teachers and students sign in with their school’s google account. No need to remember another password!

One-click video

Add YouTube videos anywhere with DocentEDU. Just paste a link to the video and DocentEDU will automatically load the video for you and your students.


Our Story

DocentEDU was created by teachers to solve one of the biggest problems they faced. Creating quality online assignments required too many steps and was still difficult to make sure students were understanding the information.

The idea of having text, questions, discussions, and guidance as separate parts of a lesson was outdated and cumbersome. We needed a modern tool that let teachers guide students through online material just as if the teacher was sitting right next to the student. DocentEDU combines the essential ingredients of a good lesson in a natural and easy to use way never before possible.
