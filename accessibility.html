Accessibility

Ways to make online lessons more accessible

Different Types of Media

Embed different tools such as EdPuzzle, Thinglink, eMaze to reach different learning styles. Our Tools to Embed page has a full list of tools that work great with DocentEDU.

Guide Students

Record audio or video of your reading the text or explaining certain topics and then embed within the text. Clyp.it is great for recording audio. Youtube webcam capture is fast and simple for recording video and saving to your YouTube Channel.

Complimentary Extensions

DocentEDU works well with most other browser extensions. These can add additional accessibility features or reduce distractions to help student stay focus.

  • AdBlock Plus
  • SpeakIt
  • ChromeVox
  • Read&Write
